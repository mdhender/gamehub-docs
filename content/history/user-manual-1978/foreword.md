Thank you for buying this rule book!
We hope that you decide, after reading the rules, to play Empyrean Challenge—that is, if you haven't already entered.
These rules should be read twice.
Once quickly, to absorb the general feel of the game; and again in order to gain an in-depth understanding.

Nearly all of the bookkeeping work, including many of the necessary complex calculations, is done for you by the computer, so the game is easier to play than it would appear to be, at first glance.

A free newsletter will be published quarterly, more or less, which will: Announce game winners, rate the other players' performances, announce new games and products, answer players' questions, and discuss strategy and tactics.
Your letters or articles will be welcome.

Finally, we wish to thank Brett Tondreau for introducing us to computer gaming with his game, "Galaxy II," and for all of his advice and encouragement.
Also, our thanks to The Flying Buffalo, Inc. for pioneering the computer gaming field.
And, last but not least, we thank Metagaming for developing the science fiction simulation war game.

We hope you find Empyrean Challenge to be an enjoyable gaming experience.
